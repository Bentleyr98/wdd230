Target Audiences

Our target audience range from the mom that just moved in, to the families that visit for families reunions, to the avid hikers looking for new summits to conquer. McCall is a beautiful small town tucked away in the mountains of Idaho. This town attracts many different people who are looking for a break from the city and to breathe in the fresh mountain air.

Personas

The first person I imagine that would benefit from this is a mom with her family that just moved into McCall. She's excited to explore the area and enjoy the beautiful nature with her family and is looking for websites and information to start settling into the area.

The next person I think of that would be interested in this site is the one that's in charge of their yearly family reunion. They want to know what kind of community is there, the hikes they can do, places to stay at, campsites, restuarants, everything they want to do to make their family reunion comfortable and memorable.

Scenarios

We've got a mom that just moved into the neighborhood. It's summer, she wants to take the kids on a hike and help them learn how to appreciate this new place they've moved to. So she hops online hoping to find a hike that the whole family can enjoy.

Our next scenario is a Girls' Camp leader looking for a good camping spot. She wants to know what the locals like instead of all the touristy spots.

There's a business owner that's trying to get some traction going. They can then look up the Chamber of Commerce for McCall, join and become more visible to the community and the locals.

Another scenario is group of college students wanting to go hiking for the weekend. They can hop on the website and get quick and easy access to all of the best hikes.

Lastly, McCall is very popular for their annual Winter Carnival. Thousands of people pour into this small town looking for food and housing while they enjoy the festivities. Here, the average Joe looking for a place to feed his family can hop on and see what are some of the best options that his family can choose from.
