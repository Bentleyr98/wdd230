Color Scheme

Background

For the background, we'll use this light brown color. This will compliment well with our other colors and direct people's thoughts to nature as they are reminded of camping in the mountains.

Header and Footer

For our header and footer, we will use the same blue from our logo. This will help give the website a pop of color while tying back into the logo.

Accent Colors

Our accent colors will be white, black and gold.
